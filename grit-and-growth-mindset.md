## Question 3
### What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?
Having an **"Internal Locus of Control"** means you believe your choices and hard work shape your life. 
This is the opposite of an **"External Locus of Control"**, which is the belief that things like luck, other people, or circumstances are in the driver's seat.
### The Key Point of the Video:
The video argues that developing an internal locus of control is the ultimate key to staying consistently motivated. When you believe your hard work leads to success, you put in more effort, take on harder challenges, and enjoy the process more. You can cultivate this mindset by actively solving problems in your life and consciously acknowledging that your actions caused the positive change.

## Question 5
### What are your ideas to take action and build a Growth Mindset?
* **Say "Yet":** Say "I can't do this yet" instead of "I can't do this."
* **Value Effort:** Focus on working hard, not just on being "smart."
* **Learn from Mistakes:** Treat failures as chances to learn, not as dead ends.
* **Try Hard Things:** Do things out of your comfort zone, even if you are bad at them at first.
* **Teach Yourself:** Take charge and actively look for ways to learn new skills.
