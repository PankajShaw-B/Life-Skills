# Energy Management

## Question 1:
### What are the activities you do that make you relax - Calm quadrant?
I feel calm when I do peaceful and quiet activities. For example:
*   Reading a good book.
*   Taking a quiet walk.
*   Listening to soft music.
*   Taking a few deep breaths.

## Question 2:
### When do you find getting into the Stress quadrant?
I usually feel stressed when things get overwhelming. This happens when:
*   I have too much work and not enough time to finish it.
*   I am dealing with an argument or conflict.
*   I don't get enough sleep.
*   Things feel completely out of my control.

## Question 3:
### How do you understand if you are in the Excitement quadrant?
I know I am excited when I feel full of energy and positive vibes. The signs include:
*   Feeling highly motivated to start a new project or hobby.
*   Looking forward to something fun, like a trip or seeing friends.
*   Feeling happy, talkative, and ready to take on the day.

## Question 5
### What are some ideas that you can implement to sleep better?
* Go to bed at the exact same time every night.
* Wake up at the exact same time every morning.
* Set your bedroom temperature to a cool 65°F (18°C).
* Avoid drinking alcohol before bed.
* Avoid drinking caffeine late in the day.
* Skip daytime naps if you have trouble sleeping at night.
* Leave your bed and go to another room if you are tossing and turning.
* Only go back to bed when you are actually sleepy.

## Question 7
### What are some steps you can take to exercise more?
* Start with short, 10-minute walks each day.
* Take the stairs instead of the elevator.
* Schedule exercise into your calendar like a mandatory meeting.
* Find a physical activity you genuinely enjoy, such as dancing or cycling.
* Set a daily step goal and use a pedometer or smartwatch to track it.
* Find a workout buddy to help keep you accountable.
